Tile adhesive

DESCRIPTION

Construction dry adhesive (mixture) based on cement binder with polymers and other additives. Used for domestic and facade work when gluing ceramic, earthenware, tiled, marble, etc. tiles.

DESCRIPTIONSPECIFICATIONS

Mixing with water: 5-6 liters per 10 kg of mixture.
Solution setting time: 120-150 minutes.
Application area: 1 kg/m2, with a layer thickness of 1 mm.
Compressive strength: 2.0 MPa.
Adhesion strength with the base: 0.3 MPa.
Required working environment temperature: +10°С/+35°С.

PACKAGE

В трёхслойных ламинированных крафт мешках: 25 кг.

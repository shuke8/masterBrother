ANTIPAS BOYA

Anti-corrosion primer

DESCRIPTION

Anticorrosive primer based on alkyd binder. Used as an anti-corrosion primer on all metal surfaces indoors and outdoors. Forms a solid base for synthetic paints. Primer for metal surfaces with good adhesion and covering ability.

DESCRIPTIONSPECIFICATIONS

Mixing with solvent: 2-3 liters per 3.5 kg of paint.
Drying time: 20-25 min.
Coverage area: 40-45 m2/kg, when applied in one layer.
Required working environment temperature: +10°С/+30°С.
Shelf life: 3 years subject to storage rules

PACKAGE

In metal cans: 3.5 kg.

FAST

Quick dry enamel

DESCRIPTION

For painting wooden and metal surfaces; suitable for application on concrete, brick, plaster and other surfaces on a mineral, wood and paper basis. Used outdoors and indoors.

DESCRIPTIONSPECIFICATIONS

Mixing with solvent: 1.5-2 liters per 2 kg of paint.
Drying time: 15-20 min.
Coverage area: 6-8 m2/kg, when applied in one coat.
Required working environment temperature: +10°С/+30°С.
Shelf life: 1 year subject to storage rules

PACKAGE

In metal cans: 2 kg.

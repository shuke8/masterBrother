Saten gypser

DESCRIPTION

Dry building putty mix, made on gypsum binder with various additives. Has high adhesive properties. Designed to prepare surfaces for finishing when carrying out internal work on concrete, cement screed or gypsum plaster and other surfaces of gypsum substrates.

DESCRIPTIONSPECIFICATIONS

Mixing with water: 5-6 liters per 10 kg of mixture.
Solution setting time: 120-150 minutes.
Application area: 1 kg/m2, with a layer thickness of 1 mm.
Compressive strength: 2.0 MPa.
Adhesion strength with the base: 0.3 MPa.
Required working environment temperature: +10°С/+35°С.

PACKAGE

In three-layer laminated kraft bags: 20 kg.

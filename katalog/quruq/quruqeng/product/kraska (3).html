HAMMERED

Hammer effect paint

DESCRIPTION

Hammer effect anti-corrosion paint high covering ability, adheres well to the surface, forming an elastic film that is resistant to salt water and humidity. Hammer paint is applied directly to all types of ferrous metal and various types of forged products.

DESCRIPTIONSPECIFICATIONS

Mixing with solvent: 1.5-2 liters per 2.2 kg of paint.
Drying time: 20-30 min.
Coverage area: 8-10m2 /kg, when applied in one layer.
Required working environment temperature: +10°С/+30°С.
Shelf life: 1 year subject to storage rules

PACKAGE

In metal cans: 2.2 kg.
